# Compliance Summary for Team Discussion

This concept is intended to stay on the safer side of Twitch and German legal risk by avoiding real-value rewards, trading, betting, and paid randomness.

## Allowed direction

- Channel Points called eggs can be redeemed for mystery eggs.
- Mystery eggs can randomly become stream-only pets or cracked egg resources.
- Pets/resources/upgrades/cosmetics exist only inside Erwin Hatchery.
- Users cannot sell, trade, transfer, or cash out anything.
- Battle results give leaderboard points only.
- Subs can get a fixed extra incubator while subbed.
- Bits can only trigger fixed, clearly described effects or boosts.

## Not allowed / avoid

- No Bits/subs for random eggs.
- No giveaways or prize entries from eggs/pets/battle rank.
- No betting on event outcomes.
- No marketplace/trading between users.
- No money, gift cards, merch, keys, or real prizes.
- Avoid gambling language such as lootbox, casino, bet, wager, roll.

## Safer wording

Use:

- Mystery Ei
- hatch
- incubator
- pet egg
- cracked eggs
- stream event
- leaderboard points

Avoid:

- lootbox
- gamble
- bet
- wager
- cash out
- prize ticket

## Internal rule

Random rewards are free/earned Channel Point mechanics only. Paid support mechanics are fixed and transparent only.
